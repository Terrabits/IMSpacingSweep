ZNB
===

## IF filter selectivity
SENSe<Ch>:BANDwidth[:RESolution]:SELect <NORMal|MEDium|HIGH>

## Tone spacing
SENSe<Ch>:FREQuency:IMODulation:TDIStance <freq>

## Lower tone source
SENSe<Ch>:FREQuency:IMODulation:LTONe <source_type>, <source_port>
source_type: Lower tone must always be PORT

## Upper tone source
SENSe<Ch>:FREQuency:IMODulation:UTONe <source_type>, <source_index>
source_type: PORT|GENerator|NONE(query)
source_index: Port or generator number

## Receiver port
SENSe<Ch>:FREQuency:IMODulation:RECiever <port>

## Measure intermodulation product(s)
SENSe<Ch>:FREQuency:IMODulation:ORDer<order>[:STATe] ON|OFF
order: Intermodulation product order 3|5|7|9|-(none)

## Set trace parameter
CALCulate<Ch>:PARamater:[MEASure|SDEFine] '<trace_name>','<parameter>'
Where parameter
For IM:
  IM<order><side>O
  order: 3|5|7|9
  side:  U|L|M(ajor)
For IM suppression (dB Output Relative to lower):
  IM<order><side>OR
For IP:
  IP<order><side>O
For noise level at output:
  NLO
Original tones:
  <side>T<at>
  at: I|O (input or output)

## Disable intermodulation
SENSe<Ch>:FREQuency:IMODulation:CONV OFF

## Spectrum commands?
### Maximum order of IM product spectrum
SENSe<Ch>:FREQuency:IMODulation:SPECtrum:MORDer <order>

### Enable/Disable spectrum measurement in channel
SENSe<Ch>:FREQuency:IMODulation:SPECtrum[:STATe] ON|OFF
